# Germany Leader Guide Civ 6
Get 10 cities by 120 turns

Setup near flood plains

District planning is essential for Hansa's

District Order
Campus -> Hansa -> Commercial Hub -> Aqueduct -> Dam -> Entertainment Complex
	Exception: Swap Hansa & Campus when Industrial Zone research is done

District Placement
	Adjacency Bonuses:
		+1 Production per 2 adjacent districts of any type
		+1 per adjacent resource of any type
		+2 per adjacent Aqueduct, Canal, Commercial Hub/Dam
	Placement
		![[Pasted image 20240620173242.png]]
		1. Create a zigzag of commercial hubs on a river
		2. Place Hansa Districts on either side of the commercial hubs
		3. Aqueducts are placed around the core Commercial Hub zigzag as a complement

Build Government Plaza in Capital City

Take out City State if worth 

Tech Order
Worker Techs -> Writing -> Apprenticeship -> Education -> Industrialization -> Research Labs

Civic Order
Early Empire -> Political Philosophy -> Recorded History -> Merchant Republic -> Communism -> Globalization

Use spies to
	Get Tech
	Defend space port

Wonders
	Ruhr Valley
		20% Production 
		+1 Production form Mines & Quarries
	Oxford
		Free Tech
		20% Science Boosts
	Kilwa 
		Envoys
		Suzerain of City State gains 15% bonus of yield
		Suzerain of 2 or more City States of the same type gives an additional 15% boost
	Amundsen-Scott Research Station
		+5 Great Scientist Points
		20% Science and 10% Production
			Yields are doubled if there are 5 snow or snow hills tiles within 3 tiles of a city owned by player

Governments
1. Classical Republic
2. Merchant Republic
3. Communism
4. Synthetic Technocracy

Against Multiplayer
	Get to Industrial Age
	Take out nearby players with Bombers and Tanks
